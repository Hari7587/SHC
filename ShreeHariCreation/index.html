    * Digital marketing is the area of marketing that advertises products and services using the Internet and other online-based digital technologies, including desktop and mobile devices.

  • Television Ads

    The process of producing and showing/screening Visual ads with the intention of promoting an any kind or service is known as television advertising. The commercials which have been aired usually vary from 10 to 30sec durations.
    Cost of running an TV Ad might change significantly on time of the day.

  • Radio Ads

    Act of broadcasting a commercial on a well known Radio station, in order to promote a products and services.
    Radio offers quick lead-times, and it is an efficient medium to grab more audience impressions.

  • NewsPaper Ads

    The Commercial which appears/printed alongside on a regular editorial content of a Newspaper.
    Typically, newspaper display ads include retail/branding, public announcements, tender, public notice, education admission notices,

  • Bus Ads

    The range of the cost of advertisement varies accordingly.

    Train branding involves placing commercial on both local and intercity trains as well as on suburban trains.
    Advertising on trains attracts the most attention and highest visibility from the consumers.
